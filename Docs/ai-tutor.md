```markdown
# Using the AI Tutor

Tinkerflow's AI Tutor is your personal programming assistant. It can explain concepts, help debug your graph, and even generate nodes for you.

---

## How to Access the AI
1. **AI Chat Panel** – Located on the right side of the interface. Type your questions here.
2. **Right‑Click Menu** – Right‑click any node and select "Explain this" to get a focused explanation.
3. **Contextual Help** – Hover over a node and click the small "Ask AI" icon that appears.

---

## What Can You Ask?
- **Concept explanations** – "What's a dictionary in Python?"
- **Graph analysis** – "Explain what my current graph does."
- **Debugging** – "Why isn't this loop working?"
- **Code generation** – "Create nodes that calculate the Fibonacci sequence."
- **Learning paths** – "What should I learn after loops?"

---

## Example Queries
| Your Question                                      | What the AI Does                                                                 |
|----------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| "Explain the `For Loop` node."                     | Shows a description of how for loops work, with a simple example.                |
| "My `If` node isn't branching correctly."          | Analyzes the condition and suggests fixes (e.g., wrong comparison).              |
| "Generate nodes for a temperature converter."       | Creates a small graph with input, formula, and output nodes.                    |
| "What's the difference between `=` and `==`?"      | Gives a clear comparison with visual examples.                                   |

---

## Tips for Best Results
- Be specific. Instead of "help with loop", try "help with a while loop that counts to 10".
- If the AI's answer isn't clear, ask a follow‑up question.
- Use the "Explain this" feature on nodes you don't understand – it will highlight the relevant part of your graph.

---

## ⚠️ Notes
- The AI Tutor uses an external service (e.g., OpenAI). You may need to configure an API key in settings if you're running locally.
- The online demo includes a limited number of free queries per day.
- Your conversations are **not** stored permanently – privacy is respected.
```
